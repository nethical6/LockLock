LockLock is a lightweight, privacy-first app locking solution designed to secure your apps without requiring root access or internet connectivity. Built with modern Material 3 design principles, LockLock offers a seamless, elegant user experience while providing strong protection for your personal data.

<b>Features</b>

* **App Locking Without Root**
  LockLock uses Android Accessibility Services to detect and block unauthorized app launches, securing your apps with a customizable password or PIN.
* **Anti-Uninstall Protection**
  By leveraging Device Administrator privileges, LockLock prevents unauthorized uninstall attempts, safeguarding your app locks from tampering.
* **Brute Force Protection**
  To protect against password guessing, LockLock temporarily locks the app after repeated failed unlock attempts.
* **Forgot Password Support**
  Securely recover access with a security question if you forget your password.
* **Material 3 UI**
  Enjoy a clean, modern interface designed for intuitive navigation and ease of use.

<b>Installation & Setup</b>

1. Download and install LockLock via the F-Droid app (recommended for smooth permission setup).
2. Launch the app and set your master password and security question.
3. Grant Accessibility and Device Administrator permissions when prompted for full protection.
4. Select which apps to lock.
5. LockLock will immediately start protecting your selected apps with the configured password.

<b>Permissions</b>

* **Accessibility Service:** Required to monitor and block locked apps without root.
* **Device Administrator:** Required to prevent unauthorized uninstall and enhance security.
